The Platform

One platform. Five pillars. Infinite impact.

VYVE brings together physical health, mental wellbeing, nutrition, education and purpose into one seamless, data-driven platform — giving your workforce the tools to thrive and your organisation the evidence to prove it.

Physical Health

Movement that fits every body, every lifestyle.

Physical health is the foundation of everything. VYVE gives every team member access to personalised workout programmes, movement tracking and fitness goals — whether they're at a desk, on the factory floor or working from home.

  • Personalised workout plans for all fitness levels
  • Daily step tracking and movement challenges
  • Wearable device integration
  • Live and on-demand exercise classes
  • Recovery and injury prevention guidance

02
Mental Wellbeing

Proactive mental health before crisis hits.

The UK's mental health crisis is costing businesses more than ever — yet most organisations only intervene when someone breaks down. VYVE provides the tools to check in, catch problems early and build genuine resilience.

  • Daily mood and stress check-ins
  • Guided mindfulness and breathing exercises
  • Sleep quality tracking and improvement programmes
  • Anonymous mental health reporting for HR insight
  • Access to expert mental health content
03
Nutrition

Energy through better eating and hydration.

What your team eats directly affects how they think, feel and perform. VYVE's nutrition pillar makes healthy eating practical, not prescriptive — supporting energy, focus and long-term habit change.

  • Hydration tracking with daily goals and reminders
  • Meal planning and nutritional guidance
  • Energy optimisation strategies
  • Practical healthy eating content for busy lives
  • Gut health and immunity awareness programmes
🥗

Nutrition & Energy

04
Education

Knowledge that changes behaviour for good.

Sustainable health change requires understanding, not just willpower. VYVE's education library gives your team expert-led content across every aspect of health — delivered in formats that fit into working life.

  • Expert-led video content and articles
  • Health topic deep-dives across all five pillars
  • Manager training on supporting team wellbeing
  • Evidence-based behavioural change frameworks
  • Monthly health challenges and learning pathways
05
Purpose & Connection

Health is better together.

Loneliness, disconnection and lack of purpose are hidden performance killers. VYVE's purpose pillar builds team culture, creates shared goals and gives every individual a reason to show up — and show up well.

  • Team challenges and leaderboards
  • Streak rewards and milestone recognition
  • Social health goals and group accountability
  • Community feed and peer celebration
  • Purpose-driven health campaigns aligned to company values
