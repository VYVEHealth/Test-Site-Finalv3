Why VYVE

A place where your wellbeing comes first

We can't ask organisations to invest in their people if we don't invest in ours. Here's what it's like to work at VYVE.

Meaningful work

Everything you build at VYVE directly impacts real people's health. We're solving a genuine problem - reactive wellbeing - and doing it with purpose and clinical rigour.

Wellbeing in practice

Full access to the VYVE platform, flexible working, and a culture that genuinely walks the talk on physical, mental and social health - not just on a poster.

Grow with us

We're early-stage and growing fast. That means real ownership, visible impact, and the chance to shape how a health-tech company scales - from the inside.

Give Back built in

As a CIC, every role at VYVE contributes to our social mission. Your work helps fund wellbeing initiatives for communities and individuals who need it most.

Remote-first

Work from wherever you do your best thinking. We're a fully remote team across the UK, with regular in-person sessions to connect, collaborate and recharge.

Honest, open culture

Small team, no politics. We move fast, share ideas openly, and trust everyone to do great work. If you want to make a real difference, you'll be heard here.
