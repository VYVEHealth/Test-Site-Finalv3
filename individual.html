Help Yourself.
Help Others.

At VYVE, we believe health should be accessible to everyone — not just those who can afford it. That's why we built our Give Back programme into the heart of the platform.

Every time you take care of yourself, you're also taking care of someone else. It's one of the things that makes VYVE genuinely different.

How it works

1

Complete 30 of any specific activity type — 30 workouts, 30 daily habits, 30 check-ins or 30 sessions each unlock a milestone.

2

Each milestone earns one free month of VYVE for someone in need, donated through our charity partners.

3

You'll see your impact in your dashboard — every milestone you hit helps someone else access the same support.
