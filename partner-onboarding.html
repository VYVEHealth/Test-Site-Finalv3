Step 3 of 8

Partner agreement & commercials

Review and accept the partner agreement, confirm how you will be paid, and acknowledge the revenue share.

1. Partnership

You agree to provide wellbeing content and live sessions to VYVE members through your partner space, maintaining professional standards and the quality bar set out in onboarding.

2. Revenue share

VYVE retains a platform fee and pays you a 30% share of subscription revenue attributed to your space, paid monthly in arrears. Share is configurable by agreement.

3. Content & licensing

You retain ownership of your content and grant VYVE a licence to host and stream it to members for the duration of the partnership.

4. Conduct & safeguarding

You agree to the VYVE Code of Conduct, safeguarding policy and data-protection obligations.

5. Termination

Either party may end the partnership with 30 days' notice; member access to your space is wound down gracefully.

Draft terms shown for review. Final agreement copy is subject to VYVE sign-off before go-live.
