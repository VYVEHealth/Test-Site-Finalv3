About VYVE

We exist to make proactive health the norm, not the exception.

VYVE was built by people who understand both the human and business impact of poor workplace health — and who refused to accept that the status quo was good enough.

Work With Us See the Platform
Our Story

Born from frustration. Built with purpose.

VYVE was founded after our team spent years working in health, fitness and corporate wellness — watching organisations pour money into reactive interventions that came too late and cost too much.

We saw smart, capable people burning out. We saw talented employees leaving because they didn't feel supported. And we saw organisations struggling to justify wellbeing investment with hard data.

"We asked ourselves: what if we could prevent the problem entirely, instead of just treating it when it arrives?"

VYVE Founders

The answer was VYVE — a platform that combines the science of behaviour change with the practicality of modern technology to make proactive health genuinely accessible for every employee, at every level.

The Problem We Solve

£28B Lost to workplace sickness in the UK every year
15.8M Working days lost to stress and burnout annually
76% Of employees say wellbeing support affects their decision to stay
Our Values

What we stand for.

These aren't just words on a wall. They're the principles behind every product decision, every conversation and every partnership.

💡
Proactive, Not Reactive
We believe in preventing health problems before they happen — not managing them after the damage is done.
📊
Evidence Over Assumption
Every feature and recommendation is grounded in behavioural science, data and real-world outcomes.
🤝
People First, Always
Technology is the tool. People are the point. We design for human beings, not user metrics.
🔍
Radical Transparency
We say what we mean, show what we measure and never oversell what our platform delivers.
♻
Long-Term Thinking
Sustainable health change takes time. We're not in the business of quick fixes or short-term wins.
♥
Health for Everyone
Workplace health shouldn't be a privilege of large corporations. We're on a mission to democratise it.
Social Mission

Health is a right, not a perk.

Our commercial success funds something we believe in deeply: making proactive health accessible to every working person, regardless of where they work or what their employer can afford.

For every enterprise contract we sign, VYVE commits to making our platform available to smaller organisations and underserved workforces at reduced cost — because a healthier workforce is better for everyone.
