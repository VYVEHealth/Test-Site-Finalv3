VYVE WORK STYLES

Discover how you work best

48 quick statements, about 10 minutes. There are no right or wrong answers — respond with how you genuinely are, not how you'd like to be. You'll get your four-letter work style, where you sit on each dimension, and what you bring to a team.

EnergyWhere you draw energy — people and activity, or reflection and depth
InformationHow you take in the world — concrete facts, or patterns and possibilities
DecisionsHow you decide — objective logic, or people and values
StructureHow you organise life — planned and settled, or open and adaptable

Your individual answers and result are never shown to your employer. Only anonymous, aggregated team patterns are ever shared — and only once at least 5 people have taken part.
