About VYVE

We exist to make proactive health the norm, not the exception.

VYVE was built by people who have lived the consequences of poor health — and who refused to accept that the status quo was good enough for themselves, or for anyone else.

Our Story

Born from real life.
Built for everyone.

VYVE didn't start in a boardroom. It started with honest conversations — the kind that happen when people who've been through difficult times decide to do something about it.

The Everyman Podcast was the beginning. A platform for raw, real discussions about the challenges people face — mental health, physical decline, addiction, loss, burnout, identity. The kind of conversations most people have in private, if they have them at all. Guests like Premier League footballer Matthew Jarvis and performance expert Calum Denham helped build a community around honest health conversations that resonated far beyond sport.

"We'd seen what happens when people don't get support early enough. We decided we weren't willing to just keep talking about it."

The VYVE founders

From those conversations came VYVE — a health service built by people who understand struggle from the inside, designed to make genuine, proactive wellbeing accessible to every person, not just those who can afford a personal trainer or a private therapist.

Why VYVE

Why we're best placed to lead this change.

There are plenty of wellbeing apps. There are EAPs, gym discounts, meditation tools and mental health days. The CIPD data shows that 37% of UK employers are still purely reactive — and most wellbeing investment achieves an average utilisation of just 5–8%.

Our Values

What we stand for.

These aren't words on a wall. They're the principles behind every decision we make.

1
Proactive, Not Reactive
We believe in preventing health problems before they happen — not managing them after the damage is done. The CIPD data proves this approach works.
2
Evidence Over Assumption
Every feature is grounded in behavioural science and real-world outcomes. We use CIPD data, not wellness trends, to guide what we build.
3
People First, Always
Technology is the tool. People are the point. We design for human beings who are struggling — not for user metrics or engagement dashboards.
4
Radical Transparency
We say what we mean and show what we measure. We will never oversell what VYVE delivers or use false data to make ourselves look better.
5
Long-Term Thinking
Sustainable health change takes time. We're not in the business of quick fixes. We're building something that genuinely changes lives.
6
Health for Everyone
Proactive health shouldn't be a privilege. Our Give Back mechanic and charity partnerships exist to make sure it isn't.
❤ Corporate Social Responsibility

Health is a right.
Not a perk.

At VYVE, we believe the people who need support most are often the ones least able to access it. That's why our Give Back programme is built into the heart of everything we do — not bolted on as an afterthought.

Every time a VYVE member hits a milestone — 30 workouts, 30 habit check-ins, 30 sessions — a free month of VYVE is donated to someone referred to us by one of our charity partners. People who need it most, reached through the organisations best placed to identify them.

This isn't marketing. It's the moral engine that makes VYVE genuinely different from every other wellbeing platform on the market. Help yourself. Help others. That's the deal.
