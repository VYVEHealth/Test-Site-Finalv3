Who We Are

Built on lived experience. Backed by science.

VYVE was born from a simple truth: most workplace wellbeing is reactive. By the time a team member is struggling, the damage to them and the business is already done.

We built VYVE to flip the script. Our platform gives organisations a proactive, data-driven approach to workforce health that prevents problems before they become costly absences.

Founded by health and fitness professionals with a deep understanding of what actually changes behaviour, VYVE is not just another wellness app — it's a measurable business tool.
