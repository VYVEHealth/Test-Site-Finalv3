The Mechanic

Simple. Automatic. Measurable.

No charity admin. No additional budget. No complexity. Just employee engagement that creates real-world impact as a natural by-product.

1

Your team uses VYVE

Employees engage with any part of the platform — workouts, daily habits, check-ins, live sessions, replays, Q&As. Any activity counts.

2

30 total activities

Every 30 activities completed across your team — combined total, any type — triggers a donation. The broader the engagement, the faster the impact builds.

3

A free month is donated

VYVE donates one free month of membership to a person in need, referred by one of our UK charity partners. Tracked, reported, and reportable.

The Business Case

A CSR story that writes itself.

ESG reporting obligations are growing. Employees increasingly want to work for organisations that demonstrate real social purpose. And HR leaders are under pressure to show that wellbeing investment does more than reduce absence.

VYVE's Give Back mechanic delivers all three — without any additional spend, supplier management, or reporting burden on your team.

📊
Quantifiable social impact for ESG reporting Every donated month is tracked. You get a real number — months donated, people supported — that belongs in your annual report.
🤝
Drives engagement across all five pillars Because any activity counts toward the 30-total threshold, employees naturally explore the full platform — increasing overall programme ROI and ensuring wellbeing spend is genuinely used.
💚
Zero admin for your HR or CSR team VYVE manages all charity partner relationships, donation logistics, and impact tracking. You receive the numbers — we handle everything else.
🏆
A story your people are proud to be part of Employees who know their activity helps others show higher engagement, stronger retention, and greater advocacy for the programme internally.
