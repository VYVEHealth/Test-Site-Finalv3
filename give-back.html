The Mechanic

Simple. Automatic. Meaningful.

No extra steps. No donations required. Just show up for yourself — and VYVE takes care of the rest.

1

You join VYVE

Sign up as an individual or through your employer. Start your wellbeing journey across all five pillars.

2

You hit 30 activities

Complete 30 activities of any type — workouts, daily habits, check-ins, live sessions, replays, or Q&As. Your dashboard tracks every one.

3

VYVE donates one free month of membership to a person in need, referred by one of our charity partners. At no cost to you.

Why This Matters

Wellbeing shouldn't be a privilege.

The people who need support the most are often the ones who can't afford it. VYVE was built on the belief that genuine health support — expert sessions, community, structure, and accountability — should be accessible to everyone, not just those with corporate benefits packages.

The Give Back mechanic is our commitment to that belief. Every active VYVE member automatically becomes part of something bigger than themselves.

Questions

How it works in practice

What counts as an activity?

Any of the following: daily habits, workouts, cardio sessions, live session attendance, replay session views, weekly check-ins, and Q&A completions. Everything is tracked automatically — you don't need to do anything extra.

Does it cost me anything?

No. The donation is made entirely by VYVE at no additional cost to you. Your membership fee stays the same regardless of how many milestones you hit.

Who receives the donated membership?

Recipients are referred by VYVE's charity partners — organisations working with people who would benefit from structured wellbeing support but couldn't otherwise access it.

Does it reset after 30?

Yes — the counter resets and starts again. Every subsequent 30 activities earns another donated month. The more consistent you are, the more impact you create.

Can I see my Give Back progress?

Yes. Your member dashboard shows your current activity count toward your next milestone, alongside your personal wellbeing score and streak.

Does this apply to corporate members too?

Yes. Every VYVE member — individual or corporate — participates in the Give Back mechanic automatically. It's built into the platform for everyone.
